Feedswap - User Guide

Welcome to Feedswap!

Feedswap is a decentralized application (DApp) on the Shibarium network, allowing you to wrap FEED tokens, deploy Meme coins, add liquidity, and manage your tokens with ease. Connect your MetaMask wallet to get started!

Core Features

  • Deposit FEED for WFEED: Deposit FEED tokens to receive wrapped WFEED tokens.
  • Withdraw WFEED for FEED: Withdraw WFEED tokens to reclaim your FEED tokens.
  • Deploy Meme Coin: Create your own Meme coin by specifying a name and symbol.
  • Add Liquidity: Add liquidity to a Meme coin pool using 1,000,000 WFEED and 1,000,000,000,000,000 Meme coins.
  • View Recent Meme Coins: Check up to 20 recently deployed Meme coins, including their addresses (click to copy) and liquidity status.

How to Use

Follow these steps to use Feedswap:

  • Connect your MetaMask wallet to the Shibarium network (Chain ID: 109).
  • Ensure you have enough FEED tokens for deposits and WFEED for liquidity.
  • Use the "Deposit" section to wrap FEED into WFEED (first deposit auto-approves max allowance).
  • Use the "Withdraw" section to unwrap WFEED back to FEED.
  • Deploy a Meme coin by entering a name and symbol in the "Deploy Meme Coin" section.
  • Add liquidity by entering a Meme coin address in the "Add Liquidity" section.
  • View your recently deployed Meme coins in the "Recent Meme Coins" section. Click the address to copy it to your clipboard.

Important Notes

  • Ensure you have enough BONE for gas fees on Shibarium.
